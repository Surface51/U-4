At Franklin STEAM Academy, we create connections in our middle school’s project-based approach to education. It’s where technology-rich learning integrates core subjects, and where today’s imagination and grit solve real-world issues tomorrow.

Alongside their core curriculum, your student will have the option to explore classes and extracurricular opportunities in science, technology, engineering, arts, and math that are exciting, challenging, and rewarding.

The five pillars that magnet schools are founded on—Diversity, Innovative Curriculum and Professional Development, Academic Excellence, High-Quality Instructional Systems, and Family and Community Partnerships—all play a role in shaping students’ academic and social education.

The innovative, thematic instruction at Franklin will provide your student with the preparation to succeed in their educational careers and in the workplace.
