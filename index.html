At Unit 4’s elementary and middle magnet schools, minds ignite with the excitement of learning.

Characterized by specialized learning themes—including the arts, engineering, math, leadership, and more—magnet schools are free and open to all students, regardless of background, race, or achievement level.

Typically, magnet schools possess four defining features.

  • Magnet schools’ diversity is designed to be reflective of the broader community, instilling empathy, respect and collaboration.
  • Centered around rigorous learning themes, magnet schools focus on preparing kids to be productive citizens beyond the classroom.
  • Magnet school educators are committed to Innovative Instruction, fostering students’ unique learning styles.
  • And through family and community partnerships, students learn to collaborate and contribute to the greater good.
